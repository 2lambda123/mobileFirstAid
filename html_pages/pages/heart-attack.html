HEART ATTACK

A heart attack is a medical emergency which usually occurs when a blood clot blocks blood flow to the heart. Without blood, tissue loses oxygen and dies. Symptoms include tightness or pain in the chest, neck, back or arms, as well as fatigue, lightheadedness, abnormal heartbeat and anxiety.

Get Help:

  Have the person sit down, rest, and try to keep calm.

  Loosen any tight clothing.

  Ask if the person takes any chest pain medicine, for a known heart condition, and help them take it.

  Take the victim to the health centre immediately.
